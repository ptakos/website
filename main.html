Project

Data Analytics

Analyzing Customer and Agent Transactions on the EKO platform

For the purposes of this project, my team and I provided an analysis of transaction data patterns and customer/agent behavior for EKO, a Mobile Cash-to-Account Money transfer service. We analyzed the behavior of customers, agents and general transaction patterns during the period of December 2014 to February 2015 for a dataset of transactions to SBI (State Bank of India). Our analysis aimed to identify the root cause of loss in customer base and profitability for EKO.
